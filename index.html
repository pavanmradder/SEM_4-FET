🐔The Silly Chicken🐔

In Idries Shah's "The Silly Chicken", a town's people foolishly trust a chicken that learns to speak and wrongly claims the earth will swallow them. After fleeing in panic, they realize the chicken was joking, teaching them not to blindly believe everything they hear and to think for themselves.
